# Xyra Chat — Short Tutorial Video Scripts

Bite-size scripts for Reels / TikTok / Shorts / onboarding clips. Each is ~30–60s.
Format per script: **Hook** (first 3s — say + show this fast or people scroll),
**Show** (on-screen actions), **Say** (voiceover), **End** (payoff + CTA).

Brand voice: friendly, fast, confident. Show the product doing the work. Keep
cuts quick. End most clips with: *"That's Xyra Chat — all your customer chats in
one place. Link in bio."*

> Tip: while Instagram/WhatsApp are in Meta review, record the **live** moments on
> **Telegram** (works instantly, no review) and use the **builder UIs** for the
> Instagram/WhatsApp features. The setup screens look identical regardless.

---

## 1. "All your DMs in one inbox" (the hook video) — 30s
**Hook:** "WhatsApp here, Instagram there, Messenger somewhere else… stop."
**Show:** Quick montage of separate apps → cut to the Xyra **unified inbox** with
WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, Messenger and email conversations side by side.
Click between three different-channel chats and reply in each.
**Say:** "Xyra Chat pulls every channel into one inbox. WhatsApp, Instagram,
Messenger, Telegram, email — you reply from one screen. Your team sees everything,
nothing slips through."
**End:** "One inbox for every customer. That's Xyra Chat."

## 2. "Connect a channel in 60 seconds" — 45s
**Hook:** "Connecting WhatsApp to Xyra takes under a minute."
**Show:** Settings → Channels → **Add channel** → pick a channel → the connect
screen → finish → the channel appears active, a test message lands in the inbox.
**Say:** "Go to Channels, hit Add channel, pick your platform, connect — done. The
moment it's live, messages flow straight into your inbox in real time."
**End:** "Connect once. Reply forever. Link in bio."

## 3. "Comment → DM automation" (flagship) — 60s
**Hook:** "Someone comments 'info' on your post — watch what happens."
**Show:** Automations → New → trigger **IG comment keyword** = `info` → click the
**comment → DM opt-in recipe** → it fills the steps → save + activate. Cut to
Instagram: comment `info` on a post → the account **publicly replies** + a DM with
a button lands → tap the button → the link arrives.
**Say:** "Set a keyword once. Now every time someone comments it, Xyra replies to
the comment AND sends them the link in a DM — automatically, in seconds, while you
sleep. This is how creators turn comments into customers."
**End:** "Turn comments into DMs on autopilot. That's Xyra Chat."

## 4. "Build an AI chatbot that actually knows your business" — 60s
**Hook:** "This chatbot answers like it works for you — because it learned your
business."
**Show:** Bots → New → pick an objective (e.g. Support) → name it → in **Knowledge**
paste your FAQ / a URL → it embeds → open **Test** and ask a question → it answers
using your content, shows the source. Then **Assign** it to a channel.
**Say:** "Create a bot, feed it your FAQs or your website, and it answers customers
from your own knowledge — not generic fluff. Test it, tweak it, assign it to a
channel, and it handles the repetitive questions 24/7. When it's unsure, it hands
off to you."
**End:** "Your knowledge, on autopilot. That's Xyra Chat."

## 5. "Never miss the 24-hour window again" (WhatsApp templates) — 45s
**Hook:** "WhatsApp won't deliver your message? Here's the fix."
**Show:** A WhatsApp chat outside the 24h window → the composer shows the amber
"send a template" notice → click **Template** → pick an approved template → fill
the name variable → send → it lands.
**Say:** "After 24 hours, WhatsApp only delivers approved templates. Xyra knows
that — it stops you from sending a message that would vanish, and hands you your
templates instead. Pick one, personalize it, send. The conversation re-opens."
**End:** "Stay compliant without thinking about it. That's Xyra Chat."

## 6. "Send a broadcast to hundreds at once" — 45s
**Hook:** "One message. Your whole audience. Two minutes."
**Show:** Broadcasts → New → pick a template → map the name variable → choose
audience (all / by tag) → the live count updates → schedule or send now → the
progress bar fills.
**Say:** "Pick a template, choose who gets it — everyone, or just a tag — and Xyra
sends it out, respecting opt-outs automatically. Watch it go out live, right from
your dashboard."
**End:** "Reach everyone, the compliant way. That's Xyra Chat."

## 7. "AI writes your reply for you" (AI Assist) — 30s
**Hook:** "Type a rough reply. Let AI make it perfect."
**Show:** In a chat, type a blunt draft → click **AI Assist** → pick "Make
friendlier" → the text rewrites instantly. Then show **Suggest reply** drafting a
full answer from scratch.
**Say:** "Write it rough, then one click — friendlier, more professional, shorter,
translated. Or let Xyra draft the whole reply from your bot's knowledge. You stay
in control; the AI just speeds you up."
**End:** "Reply faster, sound better. That's Xyra Chat."

## 8. "Your whole team, one inbox" — 40s
**Hook:** "Stop forwarding screenshots to your team."
**Show:** Inbox → assign a conversation to a teammate → show availability dots →
an internal note (only the team sees it) → reassign → close.
**Say:** "Invite your team, assign chats, leave private notes, and see who's online
— all in one shared inbox. Everyone knows who's handling what. No more 'did you
reply to them?'"
**End:** "Customer support, as a team. That's Xyra Chat."

## 9. "From DM to booked" (automations power) — 45s
**Hook:** "A keyword can run your whole funnel."
**Show:** Automations builder in **Flow** view → trigger → send message → tag
contact → assign agent (or hand to bot) → show the branches.
**Say:** "Trigger on a keyword, then do anything: reply, tag the lead, route it to
the right agent, fire a webhook, or hand it to your AI bot. Build the flow once —
it runs every time."
**End:** "Automate the busywork. That's Xyra Chat."

## Recording tips
- **9:16 vertical**, screen-record at high res; zoom the browser to ~110–125% so
  text is legible on phones.
- Keep the **first 3 seconds** punchy — lead with the result, not the setup.
- Add captions (most people watch muted).
- Use a clean demo workspace (the review/demo account is perfect) with realistic
  sample names.
- For the live IG/WA moments, record on **Telegram** until Meta approval lands,
  then re-shoot the IG/WA demos for the real thing.
- End screen: Xyra wordmark + "Link in bio" / your URL.
